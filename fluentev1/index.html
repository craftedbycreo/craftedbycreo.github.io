Fluente is the solution

Fluente is the solution to a core problem when it comes to language acquisition in America. Language learning has been focused on self-teaching, divorced from community and it can often presume that you need to master reading writing and grammar before you talk. The last stop is usually talking.

Start With Talking

Fluente wants to start in reverse. Fluente starts with talking, for minds to open up to learning to speak like a child without fear or shame. Research shows that best way to learn is by practicing with a native speaker but we don’t really speak to strangers. Fluente’s goal is to help reduced the fear of speaking through a teacher/student exchange between language learners and native speakers. Since here at Fluente everyone is a teacher of their native language and a student of a foreign one, the playing field is more equal and therefore a little less scary.

Deep Conversations

We take 10 weeks of a person’s life and they have a conversation with native speakers about their lives and in turn, their teacher is learning as well in reverse. There is no book because their own lives are the books. Fluente uses games so the sharing happens in a fun and entertaining way. Fluente keeps topics fun and interesting but give it a little depth to help build relationship. Small talk does not build relationship but research shows deeper conversation do.
